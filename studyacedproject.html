ACED — Week 1: FROM IDEA TO LIVE PRODUCT

Startup SaaS Web Dev

February 10-17, 2026

How I turned a last-minute biology test into a SaaS idea in 1 week.

Day 1: Had a biology exam later that day. Used Claude to generate an interactive study plan from my study guide — it worked so well I realized this could be a real product.

Days 2-5: Named it Aced. Designed and coded a full landing page with dark mode, custom fonts, and coffee shop aesthetic. Bought studyaced.com and deployed on Netlify.

Day 6-7: Evening: Set up Tally waitlist form, sent link to friends, got first 11 signups overnight.

Tech: HTML/CSS/JS, Google Fonts, Netlify, custom domain configuration

Lesson learned: Ship fast. Most people never get past the idea phase — I went from concept to live website in one day.
